Solana Amani Rowe
Artist | Singer | Songwriter | Newark, New Jersey | Instagram: @sza

Professonal Summary

Innovative and critically acclaimed singer-songwriter with a unique blend of R&B, neo-soul, and hip hop influences. Known for emotionally raw lyrics, captivating vocal performances, and a strong connection with listeners across a wide spectrum of genres. Multiple award-winner, including Grammy and Billboard Music Awards. Continually pushing boundaries in music, fashion, and culture.

Professional Experience

Solo Artist

Top Dawg Entertainment | 2012–Present

  • Released Ctrl (2017), an album that received widespread critical acclaim, earned multiple Grammy nominations, and solidified SZA as one of the leading voices in modern R&B.
  • Followed up with SOS (2022), debuting at No. 1 on the Billboard 200 and receiving similar success, with notable tracks like “Kill Bill” and “Good Days” gaining chart-topping popularity.
  • Toured internationally and performed at major festivals, including Coachella, Glastonbury, and Lollapalooza, with a reputation for captivating, intimate performances.
  • Pioneered a genre-defying approach to R&B and soul music with personal, introspective lyrics paired with innovative soundscapes.

Songwriter

Various Artists | 2012–Present

  • Penned hits for artists like Rihanna (“Consideration”), Nicki Minaj, and Travis Scott, contributing to their commercial success while maintaining a unique voice in her own music.
  • Collaborated with major artists like Kendrick Lamar, Travis Scott, Drake, and Doja Cat.
  • Wrote and co-wrote songs for top artists, including Rihanna’s “Consideration” and Beyoncé’s “Feeling Myself.”
  • Collaborated on chart-topping tracks, including “All the Stars” with Kendrick Lamar for the Black Panther soundtrack, earning Grammy and Oscar nominations.

Actress (Voice Work)

Various Projects | 2019-present

  • Provided voice work for animated characters in projects like The Simpsons and Uncharted 4: A Thief’s End video game, showcasing versatility beyond music.

Education
